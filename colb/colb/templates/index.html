    Blockchain

    We believe that it is the responsibility of modern society to guarantee the greatest number of people abundance and comfort. Blockchain emerges as a positive alternative in an era of income concentration, the debate over the merits of growth, and the need for new methods to transform the monopolistic hold on global wealth. Blockchain has the power to offer innovative mechanisms to a greater number of participants. With blockchain, users can rely on a safe and transparent network which better addresses the problem of fallible intermediaries. Within the blockchain, individuals can exercise control over their own money and track every transaction with the confidence that each is validated algorithmically. The blockchain's utility has the potential to extend beyond the realm of finance. It can ensure greater confidence in the production chain. The process of commodities and assets tokenization* will
    • improve food source certification
    • streamline distribution networks
    • add leverage to the possibilities of investment
      and fractional ownership
    *The World Economic Forum estimates that by 2030 tokenization of assets may represent 10% of world GDP. For more information about tokenization, see Section 3, Colb’s Blockchain Landscape

    Colb’s view on the market

    We live in an era of profound change. And change is all about adaptation. The blockchain concept carries great promise because the technology which supports cryptocurrency can revolutionize the world economy. Our desire is to play an active part in this new imperative.


    Market strategy

    We’re part of a new market, only ten years old. Look at the blockchain’s potential: cryptocurrencies alone have already averaged more profitable growth than other categories of investment. Diverse areas of economics relative to the blockchain remain to be implemented, among them the inclusion of technology to define the origin of products in the production chain, including asset and commodity tokenization.

    Ventures which demonstrate solid fundamentals and outstanding possibilities are today actively entering the market. However, there is still space for the category to better establish itself. This represents our opportunity to play a serious role in blockchain’s evolution.


    Plan to reach the market

    Four relevant factors justify our market thesis:
    • new ventures: many viable businesses for us to assist;
    • profound challenges: new territories in which we can create;
    • growing knowledge base: opportunities for us
      to broaden our wisdom;
    • areas to implement with our expertise: spaces
      where Colb can grow.
